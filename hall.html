Opened in May 2023, FHDS Hall is the brainchild of our visionary chairman, Kavir Gunpath. Designed with the community in mind, our hall offers an affordable and easily accessible venue for all your events. Whether it's a wedding, a conference, or a community gathering, FHDS Hall is here to make your special moments unforgettable. Come and experience the heart of our community at FHDS Hall!

• Comfortable seating for up to 100 guests (120 max)

• Air conditioning, tables, chairs, and industrial fans

• Draped stage area and exquisite toilets with electric hand dryers

• Fully-equipped kitchen with microwave and fridge

• Jojo tank for water outages

• Ample secure parking

• Cutlery and crockery available at an extra charge

We ensure a single booking per day to provide you with the best service possible. The hall closes at 11pm with no extensions. Plus, our onsite hall manager will ensure your function runs smoothly.

Direct Bookings: Kavir Gunpath at 083 789 7970

For full walk in and walk out packages, Please Contact 1 of our amazing agents

In case of load shedding hall does have a back generator for 2 hours which is R500-00 additional cost

Hall also has a jojo tank in case of water cuts at no charge. Please note: the jojo tank will only be switched on when your function starts

Please note that FHDS and its owners are not liable for any loss or damage to any person or property, but we take every precaution to prevent any damage. We pride ourselves on delivering the best service to our customers.
